Care

General

Each piece is made by hand from materials that respond to time, wear, and environment. With the right care, they age beautifully.

Metal

Clean with a soft, dry cloth after wearing. Avoid prolonged contact with water, perfumes, and lotions — remove your piece before washing hands or swimming.

Silver will naturally oxidise over time. To restore its shine, use a silver polishing cloth. For intentionally darkened finishes, avoid abrasive polishing to preserve the patina.

Wood and natural materials

Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight. Do not submerge in water. Occasional conditioning with a small amount of natural oil (such as linseed or beeswax) will help preserve the material.

Leather

Avoid water and excessive heat. Store flat or hanging — never folded. Condition periodically with a leather balm to maintain suppleness.

Stones and minerals

Many natural stones are sensitive to chemicals, heat, and impact. Remove your piece before activities that may expose it to sharp surfaces or harsh substances.

Storage

Store each piece separately to prevent scratching. A soft pouch or lined box works best. Keep away from humidity and direct light.

Questions

If you have questions about caring for a specific piece, feel free to reach out at contact@xvarog.com.
