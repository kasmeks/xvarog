Shipping and Returns

Processing time

Each piece is made by hand. Orders are processed within 3–5 business days. Custom orders may take longer — the timeline is confirmed individually at the time of order.

Shipping

All orders ship from Pforzheim, Germany via tracked and insured post. Standard delivery within Germany takes 2–4 business days. International delivery typically takes 5–14 business days depending on the destination and local customs.

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout. For international orders, any customs duties or import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Packaging

Every order arrives in a small box with a soft pouch, ready to store or gift. Packaging is kept minimal and reusable where possible.

Returns

Because each piece is made to order and one of a kind, returns are not accepted unless the item arrives damaged or significantly different from its description.

If there is an issue with your order, please contact us within 7 days of delivery at contact@xvarog.com with a photo and a description of the problem. We will find a fair solution.

Custom orders

Custom pieces are non-refundable once production has begun. All terms are agreed upon before work starts.

Questions

For anything not covered here, reach out at contact@xvarog.com.
